From brief to back bar

How a line comes together.

A predictable path from the formula you point to, to product your patients can buy — typically inside a single quarter.

1

Choose your chemistry

Start from our refined catalog of the professional formulas your patients already use, or bring your own concept. We confirm feasibility, actives, and tiers.

2

Brand & sample

Our design team builds your label and identity while the lab produces sample units. You approve the look, the feel, and the performance in hand.

3

Register & batch

We lock INCI and claims, handle MoCRA and any OTC/SPF registration, then manufacture your first run at a 50-unit minimum.

4

Sell & reorder

Stock your back bar, open your patient storefront, and let analytics-driven replenishment keep you in stock without the guesswork.

Compliance, handled

Sellable, not just shippable.

A beautiful product that isn't labeled correctly can't be sold. We treat regulatory as part of the build — not an afterthought you discover at customs or a cease-and-desist.

From cosmetic INCI compliance to the OTC drug pathway that sunscreens require, we keep your line on the right side of the FDA so you can sell with confidence.

INCI & ingredient labeling

Accurate, complete ingredient declarations — every active named correctly, in order, every time.

MoCRA facility & product listing

Facility registration and product listings under the Modernization of Cosmetics Regulation Act.

OTC / SPF drug pathway

Sunscreens are OTC drugs. We manage FDA facility registration and the drug-listing path so your SPF is legal to sell.

Claims review

Marketing language reviewed against what the formula can substantiate — keeping your brand credible and protected.
