Payments

Wire-first, financing available, no terms extended by you.

Wire / ACH bank transfer

Default for wholesale. Order holds as "payment pending" until you confirm funds, then ships. Zero fees.

● Primary

Affirm — pay over time

Buyer pays monthly; you're paid in full up front via Shop Pay Installments. The bank carries the risk.

● On

Credit card

Available for anyone who'll accept the ~2.9% fee. Not the default.

● On

Net terms from TCF

You never extend your own credit. A bank does, so you're always paid before product ships.

● Off — by design
💡 In real Shopify: Wire = a custom manual payment method; Affirm = Shop Pay Installments; larger invoices can use Resolve/Balance financing. All real, all doable on your current plan.
